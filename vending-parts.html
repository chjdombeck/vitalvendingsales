Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about ordering vending machine parts from VVS.

Do you ship outside New England?

Yes. While we specialize in the Northeast, we ship parts to all 50 states. Shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on your location and order weight.

How do I know if a part is compatible with my machine?

Each listing includes compatible machine models. If you're unsure, call us at (413) 200-9068 and our team will help you identify the right part for your machine.

What is your return policy?

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery on unused parts in original packaging. Electrical components are non-returnable once installed. Contact us to initiate a return.

How fast will my order arrive?

Orders placed before 2pm EST ship same day. Northeast delivery typically arrives in 1-2 business days. Standard shipping for other regions is 3-5 business days.
